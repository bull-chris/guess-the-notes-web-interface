Game Instructions

  1. Connect your Micro:bit to your computer.
  2. Open our project on Microsoft Makecode and hit the "Edit" button in the top right. Then, hit the "Download" button on the bottom left side of the editor to download the .hex file.
  3. Add the .hex file to your Micro:bit drive and calibrate your Micro:bit.
  4. Connect your Micro:bit to your Google Chrome Browser, then hit play.
  5. Pay attention to the tune that plays 3 times at the start, that's your note to remember!
  6. Four random notes will be played, one of which is your tune that you had to remember.
  7. Rotate your Micro:bit to point towards your answer, then hit the A+B button to confirm your choice and move towards it to select it.
  8. Keep going until you obtain a high score, and keep an eye on the timer!
