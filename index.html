Game Instructions

  1. Connect your Micro:bit to your computer.
  2. Download/install the Guess the Notes hex file onto your Micro:bit, which can be obtained from the project on Microsoft Makecode
  3. Connect your Micro:bit to your Google Chrome Browser, then hit play.
  4. Pay attention to the tune that plays 3 times at the start, that's your note to remember!
  5. Four random notes will be played, one of which is your tune that you had to remember.
  6. Rotate your Micro:bit to point towards your answer, then hit the A+B button to confirm your choice and move towards it to select it.
  7. Keep going until you obtain a high score!
